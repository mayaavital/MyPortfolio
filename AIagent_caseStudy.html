Desgin Interventions For AI Agents

Background & Project Overview

As apart of an AI governance course, I am tasked with conducting/writing a research paper that explores a problem and solution related to AI governance. Given my passion for design, I want to explore design solutions that might help us mitigate the harm/risk of advanced LLM Agents, in leiu of explicit government regulation in the US at the moment.

Problem Statement

AI Agents, such as OpenAI's Operator, present a different challenge compared to the traditional chatbot interfaces that have captured the attention of the world and AI market. With, the extreme independence these models operate at and the possible widespread applications of these agents, we are navigating uncharted territory when it comes to ensuring user trust, interpretation, and autonomy.

This research project aims to use lessons learned from designing LLM chatbot interfaces to explore possible UX/UI designer interventions to guide the implementation of AI agent interfaces that increase interpretability & user trust, decreases bias, and reinstate user autonomy.
